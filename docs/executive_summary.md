# Executive Summary

## Context

The company has launched several AI initiatives across customer support, sales, finance, and operations. Each initiative has different goals, cost structures, adoption patterns, and business outcomes.

## Problem

Leadership needs a clear way to evaluate whether AI transformation is working. Individual AI models may perform well technically, but executives need to understand the business impact: ROI, cost savings, productivity gains, adoption, and operational improvement.

## Solution

The Executive AI Impact Dashboard provides a portfolio-level view of AI value creation. It tracks financial impact, productivity, adoption, and operational KPIs across multiple AI initiatives.

## Business Value

The dashboard helps leadership:

- compare AI initiatives by ROI and adoption,
- identify underperforming initiatives,
- understand cost savings and revenue uplift,
- monitor workforce productivity impact,
- decide where to scale, optimize, or pause investment.

## Success Measures

Success is measured by positive ROI, increasing adoption, rising satisfaction, measurable hours saved, reduced response time, and improved operational KPIs.
