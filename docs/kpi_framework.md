# KPI Framework

| Category | KPI | Formula / Definition |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Financial Impact | ROI | `(cost_savings + revenue_uplift - investment_cost) / investment_cost` |
| Financial Impact | Net benefit | `cost_savings + revenue_uplift - investment_cost` |
| Financial Impact | Payback signal | Whether cumulative benefits exceed investment cost |
| Productivity | Hours saved | Estimated employee hours saved by AI-assisted workflows |
| Productivity | Automation rate | Share of eligible work handled or assisted by AI |
| Productivity | Response time reduction | Percentage reduction in average response or cycle time |
| Adoption | Active users | Number of employees or users actively using the AI solution |
| Adoption | Usage frequency | Average uses per active user per week |
| Adoption | Satisfaction score | User satisfaction score from feedback survey |
| Operational KPIs | KPI improvement | Difference between pre-AI and post-AI process performance |

## Executive Interpretation

- High ROI and high adoption suggest a candidate for scaling.
- High ROI but low adoption suggests change management may be needed.
- Low ROI but strong operational improvement may require a longer payback horizon.
- Low adoption and low impact suggest the initiative may need redesign or retirement.
